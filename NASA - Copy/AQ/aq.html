Aquatic life


1. Aquatic life includes creatures living in water like fish, plants, and more.

2. It's crucial for nature because it keeps the planet healthy and diverse.

3. Aquatic plants make oxygen, which we breathe.

4. We rely on it for food, like fish and seafood.

5. It's a big part of our economy through fishing and tourism.

6. Aquatic ecosystems help control the Earth's climate.

7. They store carbon and clean our water.

8. They protect coastlines and provide homes for many animals.

9. People enjoy water-related activities.

10. We must protect aquatic life from threats like pollution and overfishing to keep our world balanced.
